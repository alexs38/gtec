Spirit of Knowledge Charter School, Haywood Engineering School, Worcester Polytechnic Institute & PTC

Green Energy Modeling

The pilot project is focused on green energy – the major theme of the SOKCS curriculum – and involves creating physical and computer models of a wind turbine.

Physical investigations are done using simple turbine models in each school separately and together in the global classroom environment.
