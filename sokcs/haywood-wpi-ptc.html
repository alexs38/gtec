Spirit of Knowledge Charter School, Haywood Engineering School, Worcester Polytechnic Institute & PTC Pilot Program

Spirit of Knowledge Charter School (SOKCS) of Worcester, MA, is implementing the pilot program of the Global Technology and Engineering Consortium (GTEC).

The pilot schools, SOKCS and Haywood Engineering School (of the UK), are working with higher education and industry partners in the United States and the United Kingdom on a joint engineering project. The students receive instructions in global diversity and engineering implemented for the first time in the Global Classroom environment.

Parametric Technology Corporation in the US and UK offices serve as the sponsoring partner for this project providing free software, tutorials and technical assistance.

The SOKCS and Haywood students in one global classroom being taught by one teacher via telecommunication technology.
